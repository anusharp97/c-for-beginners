Joy is from Canada where temperature is measured in celcius. 

Joy goes to USA and finds himself in trouble all the time as temperature is measured in Fahrenheit.

Knowing formula to convert temperature, help Joy to convert temperature from Fahrenheit to Celcius.

Formula: 

Celcius = (Fahrenheit - 32) * 5 / 9;


Example #1:

Input: 120
Output: 48.89

Example #2:

Input: 194
Output: 90
